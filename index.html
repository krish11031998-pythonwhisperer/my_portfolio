Dice Roll Game

  • Developed a simple Dice (Die) Roll game that allows two players to roll dice to accumulate scores in each rounds and eventual their Total Scores. The first to 100 (default score) will win !
  • Rules

      Single Dice Mode

    • Player roll numbers between 1 and 6 (inclusive)
    • The player's round score will be incremented by the number the dice lands on
    • If the player rolls the number one , that player's round score will equated to zero and passes on the turn to the other player
    • The players can also change the maximum score to win (defualt - 100)

      Double Dice Mode

    • Player roll numbers between 1 and 6 (inclusive) on both die
    • The player's round score will be incremented by the number the two die lands on
    • If the player rolls two consective sixes on either die during or between rounds, their total score will reduce to zero and passes on the turn to the other player
    • If the player rolls the two ones , that player's round score will equated to zero and passes on the turn to the other player
    • The players can also change the maximum score to win (defualt - 100)

  • Technologies and Components Used

    • Javascript
    • HTML
    • CSS
